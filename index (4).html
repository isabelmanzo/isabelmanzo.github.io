I began teaching a couple of years ago. I found my passion for learning and teaching during my early years of high school. I have always loved the idea of being able to help others reach their best potential. The key to being a great teacher is to be passionate about what you teach. It is easier for students to find the willingness to learn when passion for learning and education is demonstrated to them.

As an educator, I find that it is important for students to feel heard and safe in their educational environment. Each individual should feel confident to speak out their opinions and feel heard by their fellow classmates.I find it to be crucial that my classroom provides this exact feeling to the students who enter it. A way I implement this into my classroom is through classroom NORMS that state for students to have respect for each other and to remain polite as well.

During my professional experience, I have had the pleasure of reaching students of many ages and helping them better understand the subjects that they struggle in. Having the privilege to see each student comprehend the material on a deeper level has been so inspirational. Seeing these changes within each student has only strengthened my passion for the art of teaching.
